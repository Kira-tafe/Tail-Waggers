About Us

Welcome to Tail Waggers


At Tail Waggers, we understand that your pets are more than just animals—they’re family. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing the highest level of care, love, and attention to your furry, friends while you’re away.



Started in 2017, Tail Waggers was born out of a joy for animals and a commitment to ensuring their well-being. I started this journey with a simple mission: to offer a reliable, loving, and safe pet sitting service that pet owners can trust. With years of experience, I've grown into a trusted name in the pet sitting community.



Our Mission


My mission is to provide exceptional pet care that gives peace of mind to pet owners and joy to pets. We strive to create a home-away-from-home experience, ensuring that every pet feels loved, comfortable, and happy in our care.


Our Values


Compassion: We treat every pet with the same love and kindness we would our own.



Trust: We build strong, trusting relationships with pet owners through transparent communication and reliable service.



Safety: We prioritize the safety and well-being of your pets, with strict protocols and experienced, vetted sitters.

Personalized Care: We recognize that every pet is unique and tailor our services to meet their specific needs and preferences.



What We Offer


Daily Visits: Regular check-ins to feed, walk, and play with your pets.

Overnight Stays: Comprehensive care for pets who need extra attention.

Specialized Services: Catering to pets with special needs, including medical care and customized routines.

Regular Updates: Stay connected with your pets through daily photos, videos, and messages.

Join Our Family


Become part of the Tail Waggers family today. We look forward to caring for your beloved pets and giving them the love and attention they deserve.
